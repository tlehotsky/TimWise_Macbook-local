The people tested answer a series of questions, and then answer this question about each answer: “From one to a hundred percent, how certain are you about this answer?” What happens? On the answers of which people say they are totally—100 percent—certain, they are right only 85 percent of the time. In most services, that 15 percent error—those wrong but widespread assumptions that everyone in the company is making—is the most leverageable part of your business. Find it, and attack it.
