First: Export Kindle Highlights to an HTML File


Option 1: Export Highlights Directly from the Kindle App

  1. Open the Kindle App: Launch the Kindle app on your device.
  2. Select the Book: Open the book with the highlights you want to export.
  3. View Notes & Highlights:
    • Tap on the screen to reveal the menu.
    • Tap the Notebook icon or select Go to Notebook (depending on your device).
  4. Export Notes:
    • In the Notebook view, tap the Share or Export Notes button.
    • Choose the export format (e.g., email).
    • Email the notes to yourself or save them locally.
  5. Save as HTML:
    • If the notes are sent as a file, save or open the file and convert it to .html using a text editor.
    • If emailed as plain text, copy the content, paste it into an HTML editor or word processor, and save it as a .html file.

Option 2: Export Highlights via Kindle Website

  1. Access Your Kindle Highlights Online:
    • Visit Amazon's Kindle Notes & Highlights page.
    • Sign in to your Amazon account if prompted.
  2. View Your Highlights: Select the book you want to extract highlights from. All your highlights for that book will appear.
  3. Copy Highlights: Manually copy the highlights or use a browser extension to automate the process.
  4. Save as HTML:
    • Paste the copied highlights into an HTML editor or word processor and save them as a .html file.
